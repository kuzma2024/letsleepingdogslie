Let Sleeping Dogs Lie

A funny, relaxing online browser game – try not to wake the dog!

About the Game

Let Sleeping Dogs Lie is a playful, suspenseful browser game where your mission is simple: don’t wake the dog. Click carefully, observe the clues, and see how long you can last!

Also known by some as sleeping dog lie or the mistaken version let sleeping dogs lay, this game delivers laughs, tension, and challenge — all without any download.

How to Play

  • Tap on objects to interact with the room.
  • Avoid anything that might wake the dog.
  • Last as long as you can – each round gets harder!

Why You'll Love It

With its unique concept, charming visuals, and no-install setup, this game is perfect for a quick mental break. Works beautifully on both desktop and mobile devices.
